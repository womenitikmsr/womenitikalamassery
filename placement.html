PLACEMENT

The Government ITI for Women in Kalamassery has a dedicated placement cell that helps students find employment opportunities after completing their training. The cell provides career guidance and training to students to improve their employability skills. Our placement record is quite good, with many students getting placed in reputed organizations. The placement cell also provides information on job vacancies, career opportunities, and job fairs to the students. Overall, the Government ITI for Women in Kalamassery has a robust placement cell that works hard to ensure that students are well prepared to succeed in their chosen careers.
