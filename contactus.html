ABOUT US


INTRODUCTION-GOVERNMENT WOMENS ITI KALAMASSERY



Government ITI for Women is a vocational training institute located at Kalamassery, aimed at providing technical education to women in various trades. Our mission is to empower women through education and provide them with the skills necessary to succeed in today's competitive job market. Our institute offers a variety of courses in areas such as Electronic Mechanic, Draughtsman Civil, Architecture Draughtsman, Computer Operator and Programming Assistant, Stenographer & Secretarial Assistant (E) and Fashion Design & Technology, allowing students to choose a trade that best suits their interests and career goals. We are dedicated to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment where women can develop their technical skills and build confidence in their abilities. Our team of experienced instructors and modern facilities ensure that our students receive a high-quality education and hands-on training. Our graduates leave our institute with the knowledge, skills, and practical experience necessary to enter the workforce with confidence and succeed in their chosen careers. Join us at Government ITI for Women and take the first step towards a bright and rewarding future. Together, we can break down barriers and create a more diverse and inclusive technical workforce.
