SPORTS

Regarding sports, the ITI has facilities for indoor and outdoor games and sports, including volleyball, badminton, table tennis, and chess. The institution encourages students to participate in sports and other extracurricular activities to promote their physical and mental well-being. Overall, the Government ITI for Women in Kalamassery aims to provide holistic education and training to women, including sports and physical fitness, to help them succeed in their chosen careers and lead a healthy lifestyle.
