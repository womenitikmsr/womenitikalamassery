Admission Criteria

Kerala ITI admission 2023 is offered to seek admission to various technical and non-technical ITI courses.

    The detailed eligibility criteria for Kerala ITI Admission have been mentioned below:


  • The minimum age limit for Kerala ITI admission is 14 years.

  • The authority has not mentioned any upper age limit for admission into various engineering and non-engineering trades.

  • Candidates must have passed class 8 or 10 depending upon the trade they choose.

  • Candidates applying for the Kerala ITI admission must have a pre-degree in Computer and DPT courses.

  • Candidates must be a citizen of India and domicile of the Kerala state to seek Kerala ITI admission.

  • Candidates must have scored a minimum of 35% marks before applying for the Kerala ITI Admission 2023.
